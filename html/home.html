How it works...

Predict price movement in crypto markets.

Join an active contest for a given crypto currency market and predict if the price will go up or down. Each contest defines a specified timeframe (ex. 1 day). There are many buy-in amounts to suit every player's risk tolerance. You can enter as many contests as you wish.

Compete with others who join the same contest.

As more people join a given contest the total pot increases. All active contests display the number of participants who have joined and how large the total pot has grown.

Win the buy-ins of all who were wrong.

After the contest ends wining players split the pot. The total pot is distributed across all winners, therefore individual payouts will vary from contest to contest. A nominal fee is deducted from each pot to support the maintenance and growth of the NXTIK platform.
